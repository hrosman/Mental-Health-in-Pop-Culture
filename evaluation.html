Psychiatric Evaluation

This assessment by a psychiatrist of a 12 year old boy in 2018, shows how he has been influenced by the depiction of mental health in pop culture. It is yet another look at how deeply mentally ill characters in pop culture have infiltrated society, and influence kids understanding of their own mental states.
